The Vision of Future

This section highlights Bangladesh’s journey towards a brighter future through three key areas: Digital Transformation for a smarter and connected nation, Green Energy for a sustainable tomorrow, and Modern Infrastructure to support progress and innovation.

Digital Transformation

Building a smart, connected nation with advanced technology and improved digital services.

Green Energy

Investing in clean and renewable energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Creating efficient cities with advanced transportation and cutting-edge urban development.

A nation flourishes when it wholeheartedly embraces technology, transforming lives through digital innovation and connectivity. By investing in clean and renewable energy, it paves the way for a sustainable future, ensuring that the environment is preserved for generations to come. With modern infrastructure, it builds a strong foundation for progress, fostering advanced transportation systems and smart urban development. Together, these elements create a roadmap for a brighter tomorrow, where every citizen can thrive. Bangladesh stands as a beacon of hope, demonstrating that through vision, determination, and unity, the impossible can become possible.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

Recent News

Stay updated with the latest developments and milestones shaping Bangladesh’s journey toward a brighter future. This section highlights key achievements, innovations, and progress in technology, sustainability, and infrastructure.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh has successfully launched its second satellite, expanding its capabilities in communication, weather monitoring, and disaster management. This milestone demonstrates the country’s commitment to advancing its space technology and boosting global connectivity, paving the way for new opportunities in research, education, and development.

Largest Solar Farm Inaugurated

Date: 22/02/2025 Time: 12.25 PM

Bangladesh takes a significant step toward clean energy by inaugurating the country’s largest solar farm. This project will generate sustainable electricity for thousands of homes, reduce carbon emissions, and contribute to the nation’s goal of achieving a greener future through renewable energy solutions.

Dhaka Metro Revolutionizes Transit

Date: 12/03/2025 Time: 11.25 PM

The Dhaka Metro Rail system officially begins operation, marking a new era of urban transportation in Bangladesh. Designed to ease traffic congestion and reduce pollution, the metro provides a modern, efficient, and eco-friendly solution for commuters, transforming the daily lives of millions in the capital city.
